Extracted action steps:
- Crack the desired number of eggs into a bowl.
- Add a splash of milk or cream to the eggs.
- Whisk the eggs and milk/cream together until well combined.
- Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
- Melt a knob of butter in the pan.
- Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
- Let the eggs sit for a few seconds, then gently stir with a spatula.
- Continue to stir and cook the eggs until they reach your desired level of doneness.
- Season with salt and pepper to taste.
- Serve the scrambled eggs hot and enjoy!
